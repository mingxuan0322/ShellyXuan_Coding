  1. press your mouse and draw threads freely;

  3. press any key from a to z, then you can see the cat change a movement;
  4. simply move your mouse(imitate using a rubber) to clean the board, or you can just click the CLEAN on the left bottom;
  5. press the key 1-9 to change the weight of thread and size of snow spots.
