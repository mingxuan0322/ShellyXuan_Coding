    About "The Voice From The Deep":

    The voice from the deep is a platform allowing users to experience free combination of sounds while enjoy sounds visualization. In the p5 sketch, I will prepare 6 or more pieces of background sounds, and each one corresponds a nature object and an animation. By following instructions and exploring freely, you can play some sounds together while seeing corresponding nature objects moving with each other. You can not merely enjoy the webpage’s visualization and sounds combination, but also lay the sounds you create as a background music This idea comes from the lockdown experience. As for me, it is quite hard in dorm to avoid noises from others, especially from those talking inside or outside my room. Later, I found some special sounds like white noise, sounds of raining…can greatly cover others voice
